  • DevSprint

    A 3-hour long coding contest were participants will be tested on their algorithmic and problem solving skills

    Participants will code in relays of 15 minutes one after the other. At a time, only one member can code, while the others wait for their turns

    Tresure hunt with clues based on mobile gaming. participants will have to crack the clue and get to the correct spot to claim the price.

    Blind Coding is where a programmer works on code without running it until completion
